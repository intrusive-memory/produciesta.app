Welcome to Produciesta! This guide will walk you through creating your first audio production from a screenplay.

## Installation

1. Download Produciesta from the [Mac App Store](#) or [iOS App Store](#)
2. Open the app and grant any requested permissions
3. You're ready to start!

## Quick Start

### Step 1: Import Your Screenplay

Click the **Import** button or drag and drop a screenplay file into the app. Produciesta supports:

- Fountain files (`.fountain`)
- Final Draft files (`.fdx`)
- PDF screenplays
- Plain text files

The app will automatically parse your screenplay and identify:
- Scenes
- Characters
- Dialogue
- Action lines

### Step 2: Assign Voices to Characters

Once your screenplay is imported, go to the **Characters** tab. Here you'll see all the characters detected in your script.

For each character:
1. Click on the character name
2. Browse the voice library
3. Preview voices by clicking the play button
4. Select the voice that best fits your character

### Step 3: Generate Audio

Navigate to the **Scenes** tab and select the scenes you want to generate:

1. Click on a scene to preview it
2. Click **Generate** to create the audio
3. Listen to the result
4. Adjust voice settings if needed and regenerate

### Step 4: Export Your Production

Once you're happy with your audio:

1. Select the scenes to export
2. Click **Export**
3. Choose your format (MP3, WAV, or M4A)
4. Select a destination and save
